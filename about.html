Our Story

From the streets of Gaborone to the world

Philosophy & Craftsmanship

Stimela's Street Couture was born from a vision to bridge the gap between high fashion and authentic street culture.

Every piece is designed with the urban explorer in mind — durable, stylish, and unapologetically bold. We work with local artisans and source sustainable materials to create streetwear that not only looks good but does good.
